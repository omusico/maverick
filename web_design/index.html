About Kazinczy Inn

Hostel is int a fully renovated royal mansion built by the Habsburg dynasty in the heart of Budapest. Free Wi-Fi is available, and there is a 24-hour grocery store in the same building.

more Bed linens and towels are provided free of charge. Guests can use shared kitchens and lounge areas with TV. Free tea and coffee are provided throughout the day.

The apartment is free from bunk beds, there are fireplaces, pouffes and plants in the public areas to ensure the great atmosphere.

Our prices include all taxes, towel, linen, internet access and free coffee and tea all day long.
